STUDENT'S FUND FOR BANGLADESH UNIVERSITY STUDENTS The STUDENT'S FUND was established by Md.Aslam Hossain in the 2019 to provide services who need help.Today a group of organisers want to provide financial effort for those in our country who are in need of financial assistance for studying purpose. Funding for the assistance provided by the 'STUDENT'S FUND' has no condition or back money. Throughout the years, we have been able to provide this much needed assistance. The STUDENT'S FUND also coordinates its efforts with various community organizations in order to provide a more comprehensive solution to the needs of our students. What Kind of Assistance Does the STUDENT'S FUND Provide? The STUDENT'S FUND provides services specialy for university students who need financial help for studying purpose. Other person can apply to get this service. How Can You get Help? To get help from STUDENT'S FUND you must provide your information in 'Candidates>Add me' then you must apply with your application contained appropriate problems and signature of your Faculty's Dean. How Can You Help as a donors? To help through STUDENT'S FUND you must provide your information in 'Donors>Add me as donors' then you must apply with your application contained appropriate description about your company/group organization /others.If you have no those then you can communicate with us in 'About' getting contact numbers or emails.
