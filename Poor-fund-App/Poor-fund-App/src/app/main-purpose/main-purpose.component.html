Main Purpose of STUDENT'S FUND The STUDENT's FUND was established by Md.Aslam Hossain in the 2019 to provide services who need help. Today a group of organisers want to provide financial effort for those in our country who are in need of financial assistance for studying purpose. Funding for the assistance provided by the STUDENT'S FUND has no condition or back money. Throughout the years, we have been able to provide this much needed assistance. The STUDENT'S FUND also coordinates its efforts with various community organizations in order to provide a more comprehensive solution to the needs of our srudents.
